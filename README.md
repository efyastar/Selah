# Selah

> "A breath of fresh air with the Word of God — right where you already are."

Selah is a web app that integrates Scripture natively into your daily schedule. When a class, meeting, or work session ends, Selah triggers a beautiful full-screen moment of pause — a verse, a reflection, and soothing music — delivered at exactly the right time.

## The Idea

Most people live by their calendar. They grind through back-to-back sessions, rarely stopping to breathe. Selah fills that gap. Not as a Bible app. Not as a notification. As a moment that finds you.

The name Selah comes from the Hebrew word found throughout the Psalms — meaning pause, reflect, breathe.

## How It Works

1. Connect your Google Calendar
2. Choose your mode — Daily Word or Journey through a book
3. When an event ends, Selah gently prompts you
4. Accept and your screen fills with a calm nature background, a Scripture verse, a personalized reflection, and soothing music
5. When you're ready, return to your day

## APIs Used

- **YouVersion Platform API** — verse of the day and Scripture content
- **Gloo AI Studio API** — faith-tuned personalized reflection for each moment
- **Google Calendar API** — detects when events end to trigger Selah

## Tech Stack

- React (frontend)
- FastAPI + Python (backend)
- Google OAuth2
